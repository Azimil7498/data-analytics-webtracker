What is Data Analytics Web Tracker?

Data Analytics Web Tracker is a comprehensive web application designed to help you manage and track your data analytics learning journey. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced professional, this tool helps you organize your courses, track your progress, and manage your certificates.

Key Features

Course Management

Track your progress through various data analytics courses

Certificate Tracking

Manage and showcase your earned certificates

Task Organization

Keep track of your learning tasks and assignments

Progress Analytics

Visualize your learning journey with interactive charts

Client-side data persistence
